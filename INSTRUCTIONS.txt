================================================
HOW TO UPDATE YOUR API KEY IN VERCEL
================================================

FINDING YOUR ENVIRONMENT VARIABLE IN VERCEL:
---------------------------------------------
1. Go to vercel.com and sign in
2. Click on your project (esc-behavior-tool)
3. Click the "Settings" tab at the top
4. In the left sidebar, click "Environment Variables"
5. You will see ANTHROPIC_API_KEY listed there
6. Click the three dots (...) to the right of it → click "Edit"
7. Delete the current value and paste your correct key
8. Make sure the key starts with:  sk-ant-
   (There should be NO spaces before or after the key)
9. Click "Save"

REDEPLOY AFTER CHANGING THE KEY:
---------------------------------
Changing the environment variable does NOT automatically update
your live site. You must redeploy:
1. Click the "Deployments" tab at the top
2. Find the most recent deployment
3. Click the three dots (...) to the right → click "Redeploy"
4. Wait ~1 minute for it to finish
5. Visit your site and try again

WHERE TO GET YOUR API KEY:
--------------------------
1. Go to console.anthropic.com
2. Sign in to your account
3. Click "API Keys" in the left menu
4. If you don't see your key (they only show once), click "Create Key"
5. Copy the full key starting with sk-ant-
6. Paste it into Vercel as described above

WHAT THE ERROR MESSAGES MEAN:
------------------------------
"ANTHROPIC_API_KEY is not set"
  → The variable name was typed wrong in Vercel, OR
    you forgot to add it. Go back and add it.

"ANTHROPIC_API_KEY looks incorrect"
  → The key was pasted with extra spaces or characters.
    Edit it in Vercel and make sure it starts with sk-ant-

"authentication_error" or "invalid x-api-key"
  → The key itself is wrong or was deleted from Anthropic.
    Go to console.anthropic.com and create a new key.

"Your credit balance is too low"
  → Add credits at console.anthropic.com → Billing
    $5 credit = ~150 full reports

================================================
UPLOADING THESE UPDATED FILES TO GITHUB
================================================
1. Go to github.com and open your esc-behavior-tool repository
2. Click on the "api" folder
3. Click "chat.js"
4. Click the pencil (edit) icon in the top right
5. Select all the text and DELETE it
6. Open the chat.js file from this zip and copy its contents
7. Paste it into GitHub
8. Click "Commit changes" → "Commit changes" again
9. Vercel will automatically redeploy within ~1 minute

OR — easier — just drag and drop the new files:
1. In your GitHub repo, click "Add file" → "Upload files"
2. Drag in the new api/chat.js file
3. Click "Commit changes"
4. Vercel redeploys automatically
